Our Services

Dry Cleaning & Ironed Laundry

We dry clean or wash your items according to the care label. Clothes are ironed and returned on a hanger.

Iron Only

In select locations we offer an iron only service for shirts and blouses. You wash and dry, we iron!

Wash, Tumble Dry & Fold

We wash at 30C, tumble dry and then neatly fold your clothes. Perfect for everyday items.

How It Works

Book on our app or online

Choose items from our price list or skip ahead with "Instant Order" and we'll bring an empty laundry bag for you to fill up.

Choose your time slots

Pick a day and time for collection and delivery. Each time slot is only 1-hour long, so there's no waiting around!

Meet your driver

We'll meet you with two laundry bags; a black one for dry cleaning or ironed laundry and a blue one for our wash, tumble dry and fold service.

Expert cleaning

We'll clean your items at our nearest cleaning partner facility, then return them to you in as little as 48 hours.
